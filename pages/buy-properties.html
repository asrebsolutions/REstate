Buyer's Reference

Due Diligence Checklist
for Property Buyers

Before committing to any property purchase in the Philippines, buyers should verify the following. This checklist covers the minimum checks recommended to protect your investment.

Title Verification
  • Verify the TCT or CCT at the Registry of Deeds
  • Check for annotations, encumbrances, or adverse claims
  • Confirm registered owner matches the seller
  • Verify lot area against the survey plan
  • Check if title is reconstituted — and why
Tax & Payment Status
  • Verify Real Property Tax (RPT) payments are current
  • Obtain Tax Clearance from the LGU
  • Confirm Tax Declaration is current and accurate
  • Check BIR zonal value for the area
Seller Verification
  • Verify seller identity against government ID
  • If selling through SPA — verify it is authentic and current
  • All co-owners must sign or be properly represented
  • Check for pending court cases on the property
Physical & Legal Use
  • Visit the property — confirm area and condition match documents
  • Check zoning classification with the LGU
  • Verify no easements or road widening plans
  • Check for informal settlers or occupants
