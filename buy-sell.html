Sell Your Property

Thinking of Selling?
Let's Talk First.

Whether you are selling a family home, an investment property, or an inherited property that has been properly settled — we can assist with valuation, marketing, and buyer referrals.

Important — Inherited Property

If the property you wish to sell was inherited, the title must first be transferred to the heirs' names before a clean sale can be completed. If this has not yet been done, please visit our Estate Settlement section first.

Documents typically required for listing
  • Owner's copy of the Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT) or CCT
  • Tax Declaration (current)
  • Real Property Tax receipts (current year)
  • Tax Clearance Certificate from LGU
  • Valid ID of all registered owners
  • Floor plan or subdivision plan (if available)
  • Photos of the property

We will advise you on any additional requirements specific to your property and transaction type.

Bank Loan Assistance

Need Help with a
Home Loan?

Navigating the bank loan process — especially for higher-value properties — can be time-consuming and confusing. We assist buyers in identifying the right bank, preparing loan documents, and understanding the terms before they sign.

How we assist
  • Referral to partner banks with competitive rates
  • Guidance on standard borrower documentation requirements
  • Basic affordability assessment before formal application
  • Assistance coordinating between buyer, seller, and bank
Typical documents required by banks
  • Valid government IDs (2 forms)
  • Certificate of Employment and Income Tax Return (ITR)
  • Bank statements — last 3–6 months
  • Proof of billing address
  • Property documents: TCT, Tax Declaration, floor plan
  • OFW: Employment contract, proof of remittance, agency certification
We Refer — We Do Not Lend

ALAS Property Solutions does not provide financing. We refer qualified buyers to our network of bank partners. Final loan approval decisions are made solely by the bank based on their own credit assessment.
